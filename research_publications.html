Research & Publications

Entrepreneurs Associates (EA) publishes Katalyst, a bimonthly magazine to share stories on entrepreneurship and programs undertaken by the organization. The Katalyst team is led by Ms. Neikule Doulo as the head editor. As part of the bimonthly publications, Katalyst conducts a research on various topics and issues through random sample selection of participants.
