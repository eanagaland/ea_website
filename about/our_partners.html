Our Partners

Alone we can do so little,
together we can do so much.
~Helen Keller

Partnerships have become an important cog in creating bigger and meaningful impacts. We, at EA, could not have achieved the recognition and success without the trust and support of our partners. We are grateful to all the organisations, well wishers, and members of the EA family.

Some of the EA partners (2016-17) are:

  • Tata Trust
  • Axis Bank Foundation
  • Northeast Development Finance Cooperation Department (NEDFi)
  • North East Initiative Development Agency (NEIDA)
  • Nagaland State Rural Livelihood Mission (NSRLM), Govt. of Nagaland
  • The Nagaland State Cooperative Bank
  • Resource Alliance India
  • Department of Underdeveloped Areas (DUDA), Government of Nagaland

Write to us for partnerships and collaborations at eanagaland@gmail.com.
